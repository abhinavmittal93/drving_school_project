INTENSIVE COURSES

These are available for pupils wishing to fast track their lessons. These are usually spread over a short period of time with a driving test at the end of the course.

TEACHING AIDS

To help pupils learn and understand the concept of driving. We have driving videos, manuals and a Rota Pad in car mechanical simulator to improve steering techniques.

PRACTICAL TESTS

Advice on booking tests and procedures can be obtained from the instructor, on our useful information page or by contacting us.


DOOR TO DOOR PICK UP

Pupils can arrange with the office or instructor to be picked up and dropped off from their home, or any other meeting point within an accessible radius.

ADVICE ON LICENCES

Advice on foreign licences can be obtained from the instructor, on our useful information page or by contacting us.

MOTORWAY LESSONS

Motorway lessons are available seven days a week and can be conducted in the instructor or the pupils own car.
